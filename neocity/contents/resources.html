Even with google I can get lost surfing the web looking for resources. This section will serve as a little collection of links to stuff that I personally really find really useful sometimes.

w3schools.

w3schools is a massive website with all sorts of coding resources and tools, even if you're not looking for it specifically you'll eventually find it whilst looking for a fix to some bug or snippet of code.

It has resources for pretty much any language you can get hired for. Javascript, React, C++. Hell, they have Rust docs!!
There arent really that many websites that have all that just centralized for anyone to use and yet, they keep the website running.

Good job w3schools, you've saved me more times than I could count.

wikipedia.

we all know what wikipedia is right? Free access to some of the world's most accurate information, thousands of editors, the whole mix. and yet sometimes I forget it exists whilst researching something? Im just putting it here so future me doesn't forget about it.
