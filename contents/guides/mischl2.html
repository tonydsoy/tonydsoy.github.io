im bored and want something to write about some tech past me discovered whilst playing old valve games.

save deletion

this tech will let you reset any level to as if you had just loaded into it from the map command

1. make a save
2. load that save
3. delete that save
4. run reload command in the dev console OR get killed by something.

accelerated back hopping

go fast on the highway chapters

1. sprint forward in the direction you would like to go. (use W key)
2. jump and turn around WITHOUT pressing the forward or backward movement keys (release W when you jump)
3. crouch bhop like you would in half life 1 but the airaccelerate is WAY low.

tuddah you are now going mach 20

accelerated side hopping

go a little slower but more visibility on the highway chapters

1. sprint forward in the direction you would like to go. (use W key)
2. jump and turn 90 degrees to the right (or left) without pressing ANY movement keys.
3. depends
if you turned right, hold A and S
if you turned left, hold D and S

do not mark the span key overlap as an issue this is exactly as i intended it to be.
